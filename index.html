About Us

Born From Fire

Where bold flavour meets open flame cooking

Urban Flames is a modern grill restaurant inspired by the energy of the city and the power of open fire. We specialize in flame-grilled dishes crafted with bold spices, fresh ingredients, and a passion for unforgettable taste. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite or sharing a meal with friends, Urban Flames delivers flavor you can feel.
